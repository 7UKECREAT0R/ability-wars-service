# Unban Player

<primary-label ref="bot-command"/>
<secondary-label ref="staff-only-command"/>

Unbans a player from the game, allowing them to join again. Requires permission to unban in-game.

## Usage

- `/aw-unban <user: Player>`

### Parameter Details:

- [`<user: Player>`](Parameter-Types.md#player) The player to unban.
