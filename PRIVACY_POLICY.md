# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Ability Warden ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and stores your data.
By using our services, including interacting with the bot or participating in our Discord community, you agree to
the terms described in this document.

### 1. What Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary for the operation of the bot, game moderation, and providing support to our
users. This includes:

- **Discord Information**: Your unique Discord User ID, and the IDs of channels where tickets are opened.
- **Roblox Information**: Your Roblox User ID and Username.
- **In-Game Statistics**: Data related to your Ability Wars gameplay, such as punch counts and owned gamepasses.
- **Moderation Records**: Information regarding bans, unbans, and blacklists, including reasons, timestamps, and the
  staff members involved.
- **Support Ticket Content**: Any information you provide when opening a support ticket, such as:
    * Responses to forms (e.g., rule broken, accused player's username).
    * Evidence provided (e.g., links to videos/images, descriptions of incidents).
    * Files attached to tickets (which may be forwarded to internal storage channels).
    * Messages sent within the private ticket channels.
- **Application Data**: Information provided in special applications, such as social media links for Content Creator
  applications or purchase details for data loss reports.

### 2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

- **Moderation**: To identify and punish rule-breakers and to keep a history of past infractions.
- **Support**: To process and resolve your requests, reports, and applications.
- **Internal Tracking**: To ensure staff actions are accountable and consistent.

### 3. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored on our private servers. We implement reasonable measures to protect this data from
unauthorized access. Access to sensitive moderation and ticket data is restricted to authorized staff members only.

### 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention**: Your data remains on our servers as long as it is needed for moderation and historical record-keeping.
- **Automatic Deletion**: Most data associated with your identity will be effectively rendered inaccessible if you
  delete your Discord account.
- **Manual Deletion**: In extreme cases or for specific privacy concerns, you may reach out to a project maintainer to
  request a manual wipe of your data from our systems.

### 5. Opt-Out and Consent

By interacting with our services (e.g., using slash commands, clicking buttons, or opening tickets), you consent to the
collection and storage of your data as described in this policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain
from using the bot's features. Every interaction with the bot will always be intentional/voluntary.

### 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and continued use
of the bot signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.
